What is MyTrickster?

MyTrickster is an upcoming open-world action MMORPG game. Embark on an epic journey in discovering the lands created by the Archons of Veinmyst. Explore the lands with any method, whether flying across the bright blue sky, or swimming through crystal-clear waters, or climbing tall mountains. Unlock weapons, equipments, and the other fighting methods available in MyTrickster. Experiment and combine these fighting methods to become the greatest adventurer across Veinmyst. Enjoy it all alone as a noble adventurer or band together with your friends.
