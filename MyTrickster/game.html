Game Features

An Epic Fantasy Adventure

Experience an immersive single-player campaign. Embark on a journey and unravel the mysteries of Veinmyst. Clear away the fog of doubts and you may understand your own origin.

Your World to Discover

Fly across the open sky, swim through crystal-clear waters, and climb large and towering mountains. Stray off the beaten path to discover all the hidden secrets of a world full of wonder and mystery.

Travel Alone or Battle Together

Charge head straight into battles alone, or join arms with other travelers all around the world. Fight the evil that is encroaching Veinmyst

Master the Elements

Not just another monotone hack and slash. Manipulating the elements in the world and incorporating it into your arsenal is the key to defeating powerful foes and solving mysteries.
